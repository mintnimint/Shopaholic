Shopaholic
====
  This is a program for helps to manage the costs of shopping, Help calculate the discounted price
and shows the results in the balance after deducting all expenses out.

How to use
- Open the program
- Click "NEW ORDER" to add product price, discount(%), quantity and click "ADD"
- Click "DELETE ORDER" when you want to delete your old order
- Click "CHECK TOTAL" to show your lists
- Click "EXIT" to end program
